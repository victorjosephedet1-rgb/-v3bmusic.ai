Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how V3B Music AI collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, UK DPA 2018, and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use V3B Music AI, we collect information you directly provide:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, profile picture, artist name, location
  • Payment Information: Banking details (via Stripe Connect), cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Content Data: Music files, metadata, descriptions, tags, pricing information
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, dispute information

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Platform:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, search queries
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session data, preferences, analytics (see Cookie Policy)
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, sales records, royalty calculations

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe for payment verification and fraud prevention
  • Authentication Services: When you connect third-party accounts
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance: To provide services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve services, prevent fraud, ensure security
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations
  • Consent: When specifically obtained for optional features

2.2 Purposes of Data Use

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Account management, content hosting, transactions, licensing
  • Process Payments: Handle purchases, distribute royalties, manage payouts
  • Communication: Send transactional emails, support responses, important updates
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, protect intellectual property
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage, develop features, optimize performance
  • Legal Compliance: Respond to legal requests, enforce Terms of Service
  • Marketing: Send promotional content (with your consent, opt-out available)

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 We Share Information With:

  • Other Users: Public profile information, content listings, reviews
  • Service Providers: Hosting (Supabase), payments (Stripe), analytics
  • Payment Partners: Stripe Connect for seller onboarding and payouts
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

3.2 We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your private content without permission
  • Use your music files for any purpose other than providing services
  • Disclose payment details beyond what's necessary for transactions

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Database Security: Encrypted storage, access controls, Row Level Security
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing, session management
  • Payment Security: PCI-DSS compliant via Stripe
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Access Controls: Limited staff access on need-to-know basis

4.2 Your Responsibility

You must:

  • Keep your password secure and confidential
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Notify us immediately of any security breaches
  • Log out of shared devices

4.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by GDPR and applicable laws.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 GDPR Rights (EU/UK Users)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for optional processing
  • Lodge a Complaint: File complaint with supervisory authority (ICO in UK)

5.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

5.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: info@v3bmusic.ai with "Data Rights Request" in the subject line.

We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA). We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Retention Periods

We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Active Accounts: While your account is active
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (tax and legal compliance)
  • Content Metadata: While content is available or licensed
  • Support Records: 3 years after resolution
  • Marketing Data: Until you opt-out or object

6.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is deleted within 30 days
  • Transaction records retained for legal compliance
  • Sold content licenses remain valid for buyers
  • Public content may be removed or anonymized

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

7.1 Types of Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, security, basic functionality
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences, language settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how users interact with the Platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Track effectiveness of campaigns (with consent)

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings (block or delete cookies)
  • Our cookie consent banner (opt-out of non-essential cookies)
  • Account settings (disable certain tracking features)

Note: Disabling essential cookies may impact platform functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

8.1 Data Location

Your data may be stored and processed in:

  • European Union (primary data centers)
  • United Kingdom
  • United States (with appropriate safeguards)

8.2 Transfer Mechanisms

For transfers outside EU/EEA, we use:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by EU Commission
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Service providers with GDPR-compliant practices

9. Children's Privacy

V3B Music AI is not intended for users under 18 years of age (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately and terminate the account.

If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact: info@v3bmusic.ai with "Child Privacy Concern" in the subject line.

10.2 Integrated Services

We integrate with third-party services:

  • Contact: info@v3bmusic.ai with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line
