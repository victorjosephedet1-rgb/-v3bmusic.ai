1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve functionality, and provide analytics.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the Platform to function:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and performance

You cannot disable these cookies.

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • Language preferences
  • Display settings
  • Recently viewed items

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Platform:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error tracking and debugging

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your activity for advertising purposes:

  • Ad campaign effectiveness
  • Personalized content recommendations
  • Social media integration

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser settings
  • Our cookie consent banner (appears on first visit)
  • Account settings on the Platform

Note: Blocking certain cookies may impact your experience on V3B Music AI.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Analytics Tools: Usage tracking (anonymized)
